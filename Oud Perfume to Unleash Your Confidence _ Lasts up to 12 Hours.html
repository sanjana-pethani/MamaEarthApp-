  1. What is Oud Perfume?

    2. Oud scents have a strong, smokey, and sensual aroma. As a result, they have a valuable status on vanity shelves and attract exorbitant prices. Oud (or Oudh) is a rare chemical made from agar tree extracts. The tropical tree produces a rich, aromatic resin as a defense strategy against the mold phialophora parasite. The source of oud is the resin-embedded heartwood. The agarwood is unique due to its age. It grows more precious as it ages. As a result, oud is an extremely rich and complex fragrance that stays long after it has been applied or burned. It is warm, musky, and somewhat sweet.
